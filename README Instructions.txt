Instructions:

1.Place images of people who you wished to be recognized in the "Known Faces" folder, accessable by the "Open Known Faces" Button.

2.Name the images the name of the person they show.

3. Camera 1 (Top left) with automatically access the devices default camera, so you can go ahead and click start. Others can be configured in the settings page.

4. Click the record button to save the security footage locally. In the settings you can change this to a timeframe, or continuosly saved at intervals.

5. On the left hand side you can select a date, or display all to see everyone spotted on the camera.

6. Selected a log entry will display a photo of who was spotted on camera
